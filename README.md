# 🌆 The Ordinary Day

> *Monday. November 14th. Just another ordinary day — or is it?*

A browser-based 3D choice-driven story game where one phone call changes everything.

---

## 📖 Story

You wake up like any other Monday morning. Turn off the alarm. Eat breakfast. Head to work.

But today is different.

Your friend **Marcus** calls and invites you to rob a bank. What you decide in the next few hours will determine your fate. Every choice matters. Not everyone survives.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

- Explore a 3D raycasted world from a first-person perspective
- Make story choices that shape the outcome
- Answer phone calls and respond to events in real time
- Multiple endings depending on your decisions
- Can you make it out alive?

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎲 **Choice-based narrative** — your decisions drive the story
- 🏠 **3D raycasting engine** — built from scratch in pure JavaScript
- 📱 **Mobile friendly** — joystick controls for touch screens
- 🔊 **Dynamic audio system** — atmospheric sounds and effects
- 📺 **Multiple scenes** — bedroom, streets, and more
- ☎️ **Live phone call system** — Marcus can call you anytime
- 💀 **Multiple endings** — survive or don't

---

## 🕹️ Controls

| Action | PC | Mobile |
|---|---|---|
| Move | `W A S D` or Arrow Keys | Left joystick |
| Look | Mouse | Right touch pad |
| Interact | `E` or `Space` | TAP button |
| Fullscreen | `F` | ⛶ FULL button |
| Debug | `D` | — |

---

## 🚀 How to Play

1. Clone or download the repository
2. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
3. Click **[ BEGIN ]**
4. Survive the day

No installation needed. No dependencies. Pure browser game.

---

## 🛠️ Built With

- Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- HTML5 Canvas (raycasting renderer)
- CSS3 animations
- Web Audio API
